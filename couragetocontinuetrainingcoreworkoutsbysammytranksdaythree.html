DAY01
Standing In and Out
12—15 REPS
Standing straight up with your feet together, bend over at the waist and touch your toes then return to the starting position. Then simultaneously jump, kick your feet out (a little further than shoulder width apart) while bending over at the waist, reach through your legs. Jump and close your feet together and return to the starting position.
High Knees
12—15 REPS
Act out a running motion for 45 seconds. Be sure to drive your knees upward as high as you can. To ensure that we drive our knees high enough, we can use our hands as a guide as see in the video.
Windmills
12—15 REPS
Bend over at the waist with our feet widened. With the one hand reach and touch the opposite ankle. Be sure to squeeze our core, and breath throughout this exercise.

Wall Crunch
20 REPS
With your feet firm on the wall, start with your hands (or weight you choose) above your head. Reach up and touch the wall (with the weight), then return to the starting position. Stay under control, do not worry about the speed of this exercise.
Leg Swings (lateral)
12—15 REPS
Laying on your back, hands firmly pressed on the ground, feet in the air, we will swing our legs from left to right with great control.
Around The World
12—15 REPS
Laying on our back, starting on our right shoulder we lift up and perform a slight "crunch" motion then roll over to our left shoulder and continue the counter-clockwise motion. Start on your left shoulder and repeat these steps, rotating clockwise.

PLANK W/ CRUNCH
12—15 REPS
Starting with a front plank, we will swing our right shoulder towards our right foot, while driving our right knee upward towards our shoulder then back to the Front Plank position. Then perform the same motion on the left side. Left to right is 1 rep.
High Knees
20 REPS
Starting with your hands and feet on the ground, jump to bring your feet as close to your hands as possible. Then jump back to the starting position.
