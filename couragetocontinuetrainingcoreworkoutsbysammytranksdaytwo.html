DAY02
Tuck Jumps
12—15 REPS
From a stand still position, we will jump as high as we can while swinging our legs up towards our chest. Maximize knee drive and be sure to land softly.
Lying Medicine Ball Crunch
12—15 REPS or AMAP
Laying on our back with a medicine ball between our legs, squeeze the ball between your knees while you straighten out your legs then return to the starting position. Any lower back pain or discomfort, place your hands underneath your butt for added support.
Heel Touch Holds
12—15 REPS
Lay on your back with your hands extended at your side and out your feet in the air. We will perform a "crunch" motion and hold it for 2-3 seconds each rep. Remember to breathe!

DAY02
V-Ups
15 REPS
Start sitting on your butt with your feet extended out in front of you. Lean back slightly with your hands firm on the ground. Drive your knees to your chest and back to the starting position.
Laying Crunch to Butt Lift
15 REPS
Laying on your back, swing your knees to your chest then kick your feet in the air; limiting your butt off of the ground. Reverse that sequence to get back to the starting point. Any lower back pain or discomfort, place your hands underneath your butt for added support.
Decline Sit Ups
12—15 REPS LM
If you have a decline bench at your disposal, perform an “old school” full sit up with the weight or medicine ball pressed firm against your chest. If not, you owe me 10 sit ups.

DAY02
Hands2Heaven
20—25 REPS
Laying flat on the ground, hands extended above your face, lift your shoulder blades off of the ground to create an upper "crunch".
Hands2Heaven (45 degrees)
20—25 REPS
Laying flat on the ground, hands extended above your face, lift your shoulder blades off of the ground and drive the plate towards your knees at a 45 degree angle to create a full abdominal "crunch".
Sliders
20—25 REPS
Laying flat on your back with your hands placed at the top of your thighs / groin, slide your hands up your thighs as high as you can go.
